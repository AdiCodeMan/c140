AI PING PONG GAME





Instructions

  1. You want to deflect the ball by going in front of the ball by moving your cursor up which will automatically hit it to the other side
  2. Each time you hit the ball it get faster but the width and height won't which will make the game harder after sometime
  3. And if u want to restart click the restart button which will restart your game. Have fun!
